How we work

Six service pillars

01

Concept

Starting from a sketch, a brief, or just an occasion. Bring us nothing more than the moment you want to mark.

02

Design

Working drawings, material samples, ready to sign off. Or we work straight to your designer’s specification if the design is already locked.

03

Foundations & Structure

For pieces that need to stand for centuries. We co-ordinate foundation design, ground works and structural engineering.

04

Material Sourcing

Direct from quarry. Welsh slate, Portland limestone, granite, marble and beyond. Provenance documented for every piece.

05

Craft & Production

Hand-cut, photo-blasted, gilded, polished, honed, riven — every finish, every technique, in our Cardiff workshop.

06

Install

On-site, anywhere. Site survey, foundation prep, lifting, fixing, finishing — UK and international. Or supply-only.

Our divisions

Five workshops. One standard.

Stone Studio is the parent workshop behind five specialist stone divisions. Each branch serves a distinct market — from landscape to ceremonial, signage to memorial — but every piece leaves the same Cardiff workshop, held to the same 200-year standard.
