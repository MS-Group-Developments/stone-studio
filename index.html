Stone Sign Co. has been quietly making the plaques, signs and stone features that mark Britain's most important moments since 1821. Stone Studio is where that craft meets its next chapter.

We work with architects, designers, developers, institutions and governments — picking up each project at whatever stage you need, and handing it back wherever you want.

How we work

From concept to install —
or any stage in between.

01

Concept

Starting from a sketch, a brief, or just an occasion.

From a napkin sketch to the seed of a sculptural idea. We work alongside clients, architects and designers to shape what a finished piece could be — character, scale, material, intent. Bring us nothing more than the moment you want to mark.

02

Design

Working drawings, material samples, ready to sign off.

We turn a concept into a buildable design — drawings, samples, proofs, costed and finalised. Or we work straight to your designer's specification if the design is already locked.

03

Foundations

For pieces that need to stand for centuries.

For monoliths, large boulders and architectural-scale work, we co-ordinate foundation design, ground works and structural engineering — briefing your engineer, or bringing in ours.

04

Material Sourcing

Direct from quarry. Welsh slate, Portland limestone and beyond.

Hand-picked at quarry. Colour-matched for conservation jobs. Imported to specification for specialty stones. Provenance documented for every piece.

05

Craft & Production

Six generations of Welsh stonemasonry, under one roof.

Hand-cut, photo-blasted, gilded, polished, honed, riven — every finish, every technique, in our Cardiff workshop. The work that left here in 1821 was finished by hand. So is the work that leaves today.

06

Install

On-site, anywhere. Or supply-only.

Site survey, foundation prep, lifting, fixing, finishing — UK and international. Or we pack and label to specification for your own installer or main contractor.
